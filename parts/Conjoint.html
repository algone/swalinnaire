Randomizer can be implemented to perform the following:
  Single Question Display The randomizer selects one question from a pre-defined list of questions and displays it.
  Block Rotation The randomizer will display all questions from a pre-defined list of questions. The order of the questions will be randomized.

Discrete Choice Here respondents are asked to choose between multiple products and the relative weights for each of the attributes are calculated indirectly

Ratings Based Conjoint Here respondents are asked to Rate the likelyhood of purchase for two products at a time.
Custom Scripts
Custom Scripts enable you to setup delayed branching. Delayed branching is basically programmed logic which decides weather to display/skip questions based on answers to previous questions.
For example, lets assume you have 3 options for Q1
  Q1. Please select one option:
  • A
  • B
  • C
Now say, you wish to display Q21 if Q1 = A.

#if ( ${Q1} == 1 )
$survey.branchTo("Q21")
#end
